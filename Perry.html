Aust. Ch. Eraky Dark Horse

D.O.B: 27/04/23 Hips: 1:3 Elbows: 0:0 DNA: Clear by parentage

Perry had a great start to her showing career, being awarded 11 Baby Puppy in Group and 7 Baby Puppy in Show awards under both Australian and International judges. Perry achieved her Australian Championship at just over 12 months of age. This beautiful girl has a bright future, and we are super excited to watch her mature.

13th Labrador Specialty Show

(South Australia)

Aust. Ch. Eraky Dark Horse "Perry" was awarded the following at the 13th National Labrador Show under judge Mr. Sven Slettedal (Norway):
  • 1st place in the Junior Bitch class
  • Best Opposite Junior in Show
Critique from Mr. Sven Slettedal (Norway):
"A very nice, feminine bitch in good condition. Strong and of very good type. I believe that when she matures, she will be a lovely example of the breed. She has a lovely head and expression, correct neck and angulation, and a strong topline. She moves very well and has an excellent coat and tail."
