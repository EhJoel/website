Aust. Ch. Eraky Open Invitation "Willow"

D.O.B: 30/12/21 Hips: 4:4 Elbows: 1:0 DNA: Clear by parentage

We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to Kyle Charnock & Erin Charnock of Eraky Labradors for entrusting us with Miss "Willow". Since joining our team, Willow has rapidly earned her Australian Championship title, showcasing her exceptional qualities in the ring. We are incredibly pleased with her progress and are proud to share that she has received outstanding critiques from highly respected judges. Her success is a true testament to her lovely conformation and temperament.

13th Labrador National Show 2024

(South Australia)

Ch. Eraky Open Invitation "Willow" was awarded Second place in the Intermediate Bitch class with 32 entries at the 13th National Labrador Show, under Judge Mr. Sven Slettedal from Norway. This prestigious recognition highlights Willow's exceptional conformation and quality.
