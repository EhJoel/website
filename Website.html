About Us

We are Karenda Labradors, located on a small acreage in Wesley Vale, Tasmania, we are passionate and dedicated to breeding high quality Labradors that emphasize health, conformation, and temperament. Our goal is to produce well-rounded, family-friendly companions. All of our breeding dogs undergo hip and elbow scoring, as well as DNA testing or are clear by parentage, ensuring that our puppies are free from testable genetic diseases. We proudly showcase our dogs in the show ring both in Tasmania and interstate, highlighting their exceptional qualities. We welcome inquiries about our puppies and are happy to answer any questions you may have.
